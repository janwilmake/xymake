Frequently Asked Questions

How does the free plan work?

With our free plan, you can convert any X (formerly Twitter) post into LLM-ready format by simply changing the URL from "x.com" to "xymake.com". This works for individual posts only.

What's included in the Premium plan?

Premium subscribers can create a complete public export of all their X content, making their entire feed accessible to LLMs and AI tools. You'll also get custom AI processing templates and enhanced API access.

Can I try Premium before committing?

Yes! We offer a 7-day free trial of our Premium plan so you can explore all features before subscribing. No credit card required for the trial period.

How does the Business plan work?

Our Business plan is tailored to your enterprise needs through our partner program. We'll work with you to create custom solutions for content analysis, repurposing, and AI integration at scale.

Is my data secure?

XYMake only accesses X content that you explicitly authorize. Your login credentials are never stored, and we use industry-standard security protocols to protect all data.
